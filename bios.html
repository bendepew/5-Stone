Bryan Hughes

Co-Founder and CEO of 5Stone, has 20 years of technology consulting and development experience for fortune 100 companies and the US Department of Defense. Beginning his technology career in the United States Air Force, Bryan quickly distinguished himself as a technology thought leader and innovator before joining the civilian world as a global project manager for infrastructure modernization efforts consulting to Hitachi and General Electric. Prior to founding 5Stone, Bryan served as the Global Operations Program Manager at Santa Claire based start-up Nexenta and as the Solutions Director for Resolvit, LLC in Cincinnati, OH; overseeing all client projects and managed services globally.
